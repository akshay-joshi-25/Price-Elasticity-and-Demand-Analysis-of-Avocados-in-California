# 📊 Price Elasticity and Demand Analysis of Avocados in California

This report serves two objectives:
- Asses the price elasticity of conventional and organic Avocados in 7 markets across California - Las Vegas, Los Angeles, Sacramento, San Diego, and San Francisco.
- Evaluate how changing the price would affect the demand for conventional and organic Avocados in each of the 7 markets.
